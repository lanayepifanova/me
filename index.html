Forge
the vision
Forge is a hacker house I founded in Rice Village to bring the same energy, collaboration, and intensity I experienced while living in C House at Harvard to Houston and the Rice community. The vision behind Forge is to create a space where ambitious builders, founders, and engineers can live, learn, and create together. It's a home for people obsessed with ideas, late-night coding, and turning ambitious projects into reality. It's meant to bridge the gap between academia and real-world innovation by fostering a culture of experimentation, accountability, and momentum. At Forge, residents push each other to build fast, think boldly, and contribute to Houston's growing startup and tech ecosystem while staying grounded in the supportive, intellectually curious environment that Rice is known for.
my role
As the founder of Forge, I oversee every part of the house's operation from vision to execution. I handle resident recruiting, making sure each cohort is filled with ambitious builders who embody the spirit of the space. I also take the lead on securing sponsors and partnerships, managing fundraising efforts, and organizing events that bring together Houston's startup, tech, and academic communities. Beyond the strategic side, I'm deeply involved in the day-to-day coordinating logistics, maintaining the space, and ensuring the environment stays inspiring and functional. In many ways, I wear every hat: founder, operator, community builder, marketer, and cleaning. Because every detail matters when you're building something from the ground up.
